# Privacy Policy

**Fetch - Download Manager**

Last updated: March 2026

Fetch does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All downloads, settings, tags, and statistics are stored locally on your device.

## Data We Don't Collect
- No analytics or tracking
- No personal information
- No download history sent to any server
- No third-party SDKs that collect data

## Network Access
Fetch connects only to URLs you provide for downloading files. No other network requests are made.

## Contact
If you have questions, open an issue on this repository.
